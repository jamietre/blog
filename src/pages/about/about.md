## About Me

Greybeard software engineer living in Maine. I've been writing code since before there was an internet. Have some opinions, am often wrong, but always trying to learn something new.

When I'm not at a keyboard I'm usually working on something with my hands — home improvement projects, fixing things that probably should have been replaced, or splitting firewood. Often tinkering with my home lab or trying to automate something.
